OPERATIONS RESEARCH ONE

"Qualified Practice 1"

For this assignment I chose a Linear problem about transfer. The case was created by me with the general features for transfer problems. It has 30 variables and 13 constraint. Then, the case tell us about a great company it is called Retav Digital Co. and it dedicate to produce and sell digital products to use in the learning. One of them is a new generation of 'proyector'. The problem is base on the quantity of this products we have to transfer from the factories to the distribution center and then to the client.

Every transport has a unit cost, so the main goal for our is to minimize the total cost for transfer. It also mentions that the factories are in Nóvgorod and the distribution centers are, one of them in St Petersburg and the others in the north and south of Moscow. We have constraints for production capacity and we also have constraints for demand for clients. The cost of sending this products are displaying in the pdf file.

About me

Jairo Vásquez Auqui, Industrial engineering student

I was born and grew up in Lima, Perú. I studied my primary and high school in a school from San Borja district. I finished high school with honours. Thanks to my qualifications, I could represent my school in a variety of contests and competitions organized by the MINEDU. Since high school I was interested in the science and especially in Physics and Astronomy. Although I couldn't study science, one of my passions, I didn't stop learning science. Even I have gone to many events about Physics and scientific conferences about it. And thanks God, in a few time, I could enter in first place to the Universidad de Ingeniería y Tecnología to study my first passion, engineering. I've always been fascinated with the phenoms of our reality. I've gone to different conferences about physics, astronomy, nuclear energy, electronics and robotics. All those knowledge help me now for all my courses in the university. I've worked like programmer in Open Health (OP) the last summer, it is an StarUp supported by the UTEC Business accelerator Utec ADVENTURES where I made the primary scheme of front-end of the mobile application.

Since I started to study in UTEC. I've tried to study so hard and develop projects. One of them was "Develop an app for OS Android to measure and control the use of water in homes". I could measured in real time water consumption in a home and it could display it in the mobile application. A functional prototype that met the expectations (see more). Other project I had was "Development of eco-friendly clothes that do not need to wash". We were able to structure the process for obtaining nitrogen nanoparticles, the main component in fotocatalización on the garment. A functional prototype will be the next step (see more).

I have advised reinforcement to students in the first cycles courses: calculus, general chemistry, general physics and programming. These days, I advise in the course of calculation.Currently, I am in the first place of my carrer, Industrial Engineering. In my free time I read books or magazines about economy and Economic History. Now I am interested in georeferenced systems and this cycle I will develop a project based on this (see more). I invite you to read THE CRITICAL ENGINEERING MANIFESTO, I hope you enjoy it.

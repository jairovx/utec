OPERATIONS RESEARCH ONE

"Qualified Practice 3"

For this assignment we had to interpret a problem about Aggregate Planning. The case was taken by Chase y Aquilano, “Fundamentals of Operations Management”, Irwin Pub., 1991. It has 47 variables and 25 constraint. Then, the case tell us about a great company of electrodomestics and it dedicate to produce and sell wash machines products in five types of items. We have to solve the problem with the constraint for workers, production costs, hiring costs, firing costs, Labor costs, backlog costs and overtime costs'. The problem is based on minimize the total costs for the production using the Lingo software.
